🚢El barco en resumen

The lead ship of Holland America's flagship Pinnacle Class, christened by Queen Máxima of the Netherlands, Koningsdam pairs a music-and-design theme with the line's most ambitious dining and entertainment line-up. Named decks honour composers — Beethoven, Gershwin, Mozart, Schubert — and the ship centres on the two-story World Stage theatre and the multi-venue Music Walk. A 2026 dry dock refreshed the live-music spaces.

🧭
Para quién es

Aimed squarely at Holland America's core: mature, well-travelled guests who choose a premium mid-size ship for its culinary depth and live-music program over waterslides and crowds. A strong fit for couples, music lovers and multi-generational family groups who still want a refined, unhurried atmosphere.

🗺️Dónde y cuándo navega

Officially listed on Holland America's Alaska program (summer, roundtrip ex-Seattle and Vancouver). Typically repositions to Mexican Riviera / Pacific ex-San Diego in winter — verify current season.
