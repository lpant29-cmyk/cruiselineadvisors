    🚢The ship at a glance

    Queen Victoria carries Cunard's White Star Service and ocean-liner heritage in an intimate, classically styled ship — evoking the grandeur of golden-age travel with a two-storey library, ballroom and formal dining.

    🧭
    Who it's for

    Best for couples and traditional cruisers who value a smaller, refined, formal atmosphere; ballroom dancers, afternoon-tea and enrichment lovers. Skews older and affluent, with a British-influenced ambience; not aimed at families seeking activity-packed ships.
